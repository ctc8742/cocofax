## What Is CocoFax, Actually?

CocoFax is an online fax service that lets you send and receive faxes from your phone, computer, browser, or even directly from Gmail — no fax machine, no landline, no toner cartridges that cost more than printer ink somehow.

You get a real fax number (local or toll-free, your pick). When someone faxes you, the document lands in your email inbox as a PDF. When you want to send one, you upload a file, type in the recipient's fax number, and hit send. CocoFax dials the line and handles everything else.

Over 200,000 individuals and businesses worldwide are using it. It's picked up awards from G2, Capterra, and SourceForge — not the kind of thing a mediocre product usually collects.
